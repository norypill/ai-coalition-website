Why This Exists

Generation Alpha was born to be AI natives. We are the springboard that helps them build what's next.

Today's high schoolers have a generational opportunity to lead the AI era. Yet, without formal resources or educators to guide them, many barely scratch the surface, rarely moving beyond the chatbot stage. To unlock what they are truly capable of, they need more than just access. They need a spark: a tangible goal they can bring to life using the power of AI.


They need a reason to go deeper. When a student decides to build an AI-powered startup, a nonprofit, or a real-world tool that excites their heart, that is when real AI learning begins. By pursuing an outcome they actually care about, the motivation to master AI comes naturally. Not from a textbook, but from necessity.


We are the accelerator that springboards that ambition. We pair students with mentors and resources to supercharge their efforts. Our goal is not just to teach AI; we empower students to build, ship, and make a tangible impact on the world.

Once they start building, they will learn and develop faster than we dare to imagine.

Who We Are

Built by NORY. Designed for the most ambitious high schoolers ready to lead with AI.

AI Collective is an initiative of NORY Changemakers Corp, a 501(c)(3) nonprofit. NORY is NYC's largest STEM enrichment organization, operating across multiple neighborhoods and expanding nationally.

Our educator advisory council — a group of leading NYC STEM educators — guides student recruitment and program design. Our industry mentors come from top technology companies and leading universities.

We believe the next transformative AI company, the next breakthrough nonprofit, the next AI-powered tool that changes millions of lives — could be built by a high schooler. That high schooler could be you.
